Features

Auto Download

You can download tv shows, movies and subtitles automatically.

 

Track Tv Shows

You can track your tv shows, marked as watched and play unwatched last episode

 

Subtitle Download

Novaroma finds available subtitle, downloads and changes titles automatically. Also you can search, select and download subtitles.

 

Discover

When you don't know what you want watch, you can search with title, rating, votes, genres etc. parameters for new tv shows and movies.

 

Tv Show and Movie Info

You can see tv show and movie info (Poster, Imdb Rating, Votes, Description etc.)

 

Manual Download

You can search, select and download tv shows, movies, subtitles manually.

 

Activities

You can see activities, started and finished downloads.

 

Add Folder

You can add your movies, tv shows folder to Novaroma.

 

Back up

You can back up Novaroma database to your local folders or local google drive, onedrive, dropbox etc. folders

 

Filter

You can filter and search your added movies and tv shows

 

Right Click Context Menu

You can control folders, video files etc. and add movies and tv shows , download subtitles with novaroma right click context menu.

 

Folder Preview

You can see movie or tv show details from folder preview

 

Script Services For Developers

You can write, compile and run new services in novaroma.

 

Plugin Support

You can write new services for Novaroma and add to plugin folder.
